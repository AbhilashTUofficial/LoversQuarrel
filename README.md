# ❤️ Lover's Quarrel

An AI-powered relationship argument simulator where two AI partners hilariously debate a topic provided by players.

The goal is not to promote toxic behavior, but to parody common relationship disagreements and turn them into entertainment.

---

# What Is This?

Players provide:

* An argument topic
* Personality traits
* Character sliders
* Additional context

The AI then generates a back-and-forth argument between two virtual partners while a narrator tracks the chaos.

Example:

Topic:

> You forgot my birthday.

Traits:

* Historian
* Detective
* Drama Queen

Result:

> "Funny how you forgot my birthday but remembered every football score this month."

---

# Core Gameplay

## Step 1

Enter a topic.

Examples:

* You forgot my birthday
* Why didn't you reply?
* You liked someone's photo
* You never listen

---

## Step 2

Assign traits.

Examples:

* Historian
* Detective
* Lawyer
* Mind Reader
* Drama Queen
* Passive Aggressive
* Sarcastic
* Overthinker
* Score Keeper

---

## Step 3

Adjust character sliders.

Examples:

* Intellect
* Logic
* Drama
* Confidence
* Stubbornness
* Sarcasm
* Memory
* Emotional Damage

Each slider changes how the character behaves during the argument.

---

## Step 4

Watch the argument unfold.

Characters take turns arguing while a narrator comments on the situation.

Example:

🔥 Emotional Damage +15

🧠 Logic Attempt Failed

☠️ Toxicity Increased

---

# Live Statistics

Track:

* Relationship Health
* Drama Meter
* Logic Meter
* Toxicity Meter
* Apology Chance
* Winner Probability

---

# Chaos Cards

Players can interrupt the argument.

Examples:

* Bring Up Old Incident
* Screenshot Evidence
* Leave On Read
* Mom Joins Chat
* Friend Gives Advice
* Found Old Messages
* Send 👍 Instead Of Reply

Chaos cards dramatically change the direction of the conversation.

---

# Random Events

Random events can occur during any argument.

Examples:

* Old Screenshot Found
* Friend Enters Chat
* Instagram Like Discovered
* Accidental Voice Note Sent

* Winner

* Logic Score
